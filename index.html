Bezeq Rotary Phone Labels

Reproduction labels for rotary dial telephones manufactured and marketed by Bezeq, internationally known as the Israeli National Telecommunication Company or B-COM.

This generator creates customised printable labels for Bezeq rotary telephones. It was designed to replicate the styling of the iconic Bezeq rotary telephones from the 1980s.
